# SINCON2025-CTF-Badge
SINCON 2025 concluded last year but I just had the time to collate the steps to solve the challenges only now. All full access pass holders were entitled to an exclusive electronic hardware badge which included a mini CTF. Comprising of 8 challenges, after resolving each challenge, more LED lights were lighted upon the board, depending on which challenge you did.

Similar to the previous years, you had to solder the LED's on the board yourself at the Electronic Kampong Village. This time, 8 different colours were available. The badge on its own is powered by a CR2032 coin battery, or a USB-C connection. Of course to connect to the PC, you need to connect via a USB-C.
